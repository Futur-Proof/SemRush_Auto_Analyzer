## Viral Content Formulas

### Formula 1: The Reveal
```
Setup: "Customer ordered this 3 weeks ago..."
Build: Show the piece in detail
Payoff: Customer reaction (genuine emotion)
CTA: "DM us to start your custom piece"
```

### Formula 2: The Education
```
Hook: "Most jewelers won't tell you this..."
Value: Drop genuine insider knowledge
Proof: Show example/demonstration
CTA: "Come see the difference yourself"
```

### Formula 3: The Comparison
```
Hook: "Pawn shop vs. CaratTrade"
Show: Side-by-side offer comparison
Explain: Why the difference exists
CTA: "Get a real offer - link in bio"
```

### Formula 4: The Transformation
```
Before: Raw materials or customer's old piece
Process: Timelapse/clips of creation
After: Final reveal with customer
CTA: "Your piece could be next"
```

### Formula 5: The Controversy (Use Sparingly)
```
Hook: Strong opinion or industry callout
Support: Back it up with facts/examples
Engagement: Ask audience opinion
Note: Creates discussion, drives shares
```
